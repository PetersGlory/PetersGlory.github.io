Welcome to I Can Academy

Thank you for choosing I Can Academy for your child. We assure you that you have made the right choice.

At 

I Can Academy
, we understand that every child is Unique and has the ability to take a positive lead in any sphere of life. With this envisioned, we are guided by a well thought-out Mission and Values to nurture every child into becoming who they are created to be. The academic program at I Can Academy is rich and engaging


Students are engaged on a daily basis using a blend of British and Nigerian Curriculum having a broad number of subject areas. They interact with great books and materials. They are encouraged to love their studies and to find delight in Godly and moral values. Through training in habits of minf and attention, I Can Academy students are equipped with the tools they need for a lifetime of learning and service.
