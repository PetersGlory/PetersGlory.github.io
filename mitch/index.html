OUR VISION

Commitment to the development of the total child
-Provide education at foundation of high standard for both infants and children.
-Show evidence of smooth transition from home to school
-Indicate by their activities readiness for primary level of education and eventually into secondary schools.
-Teach co-operation and team spirit
-Give children every opportunity and facility for all round development of their personalities.

OUR MISSION

To create the best of human resources and technology to create a centre for knowledge and character training that will produce intelligent and morally upright children who have skill and ability to suceed in a quickly changing world.

Welcome to Our School

Mitchel private school We commenced the school with the following pupils: Joy Olisah, Bisi Salau, Aminat Taiwo and Bolu Otoikhila. The first set of teachers(staff) were Mr Henry Osayi Alile, Mrs R. Mary Ann Onanuga and Christy Oyawale.


Students are engaged on a daily basis using a blend of British and Nigerian Curriculum having a broad number of subject areas. They interact with great books and materials. They are encouraged to love their studies and to find delight in Godly and moral values. Through training in habits of mind and attention, Mitchel private school students are equipped with the tools they need for a lifetime of learning and service.
