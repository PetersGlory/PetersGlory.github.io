About Us

kings bride international Christian Academy is that focuses on reducing the rate of out of school children through our low school fee thereby enhancing academic development of diligent children and enhance their productivity as useful members of the society.


King's Bride International Christian Academy was founded on the 7th of September 2015 by Mr joseph Ejodamen Iluobe and Mrs Adaku Joseph iluobe. It was a long time vision that became a reality with pre-nursery, nursery 1-3,basic 1-5 and Jss 1 classes . On the day of resumption, Kings Bride international Christian Academy started with just three kids (the director's children only),having the proprietor and the proprietress as the only teachers.But each day that came by, the population


Kept increasing and there need to employ more hands.Before the end of that first term,the population rose to fifty (50) Pupils/students.By the end of the second academic session, there was a tremendous increase in the number pupils/students because our academic input on bott the pupils and students is second to none.


    Core Values are:
  • RESPECT: We believe in creating a safe secured learning environment where respect, honesty and appreciation of individual differences are fostered.
  • COLLABORATION : We believe cultivating and maintaining partnership is essential.
  • HIGH EXPECTATIONS : We believe in maintaining rigorous standards and high expectations for all students which are keys to academic excellence and life long learning.
  • RESPONSIBILITY : We believe that motivating students to become independent learners leads to taking responsibility for their own learning.
  • EQUITY : We believe in providing all students with a balanced curriculum aligned with quality instructions.
  • PRIDE : We believe in instilling a sense of pride in our school and community.


Our first ss3 students sat for their 2021 West African Senior School Certificate Examination and National Examination Council Examination of which they all came out in flying colours.
