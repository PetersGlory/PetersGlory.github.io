OUR VISION

Through our affordable school fee services,we will continue to be in active partnership with parents and communities. We will foster personal development, intellectual growth and prepare each student to contribute and succeed as a productive and responsible global citizen.

OUR MISSION

To promote individual talents and maximise each student's potential.

About Us

Kings Bride international Christian Academy believes in creating a safe secured learning environment where respect, honesty and appreciation of individual differences are fostered, cultivating and maintaining partnership is essential maintaining rigorous standards and high expectations for all students which are keys to academic excellence and life long learning, motivating students to become independent learners leads to taking responsibility for their own learning and more..


    Core Values are:
  • RESPECT: We believe in creating a safe secured learning environment where respect, honesty and appreciation of individual differences are fostered.
  • COLLABORATION : We believe cultivating and maintaining partnership is essential.
  • HIGH EXPECTATIONS : We believe in maintaining rigorous standards and high expectations for all students which are keys to academic excellence and life long learning.
  • RESPONSIBILITY : We believe that motivating students to become independent learners leads to taking responsibility for their own learning.
  • EQUITY : We believe in providing all students with a balanced curriculum aligned with quality instructions.
  • PRIDE : We believe in instilling a sense of pride in our school and community.
